Welcome to ChapterX!
We, the ChapterX team, passionately believe that reading and writing should be accessible to everyone. That's why we've created this innovative e-book platform where anyone can publish, discover, and share their own stories while enjoying the creativity of others.

We're not just here for writers and readers. ChapterX is one of the few e-book platforms where publishers also find a home. Here, they have the opportunity to discover new authors, publish their own books directly, and share them with the world of readers. Through our platform, publishers can easily reach a broader audience and facilitate book sales.

ChapterX encourages writers to independently publish their works, making them accessible to everyone. On the platform, anyone can easily publish, and readers can freely browse through free and paid books. Our goal is to create a vibrant community where writers and readers can connect, share their thoughts, and experiences.

Welcome here, dear visitor!

My name is Vivien Brigitta Kiss, and I am the mastermind behind this website's concept. In January of 2023, I came up with a website idea, that is primarily focused on aspiring writers and their careers.

When I was younger, I had a strong interest in books and reading. Since I have been a wattpad user for a considerable amount of time and as a result I discovered some shortcomings on the site. My goal with this idea was to address these shortcomings by focusing on emerging writers and giving them the attention they deserve.

I worked mainly on the front-end code for this project, but I also assisted with the design work. Come along with me on this thrilling journey as we celebrate the world of young writers and their stories on this platform!
